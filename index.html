Welcome to the Assendelft Newcomer's Guide

Discover Assendelft: Your New Home Welcome to Assendelft, which is part of Zaanstad neighborhood therefore embraces people from all around the world. Sounds too optimistic but infact it has immigrants from such non-western countries like Turkey, Surinam, Morocco and the Netherlands Antilles and Aruba. If you are considering making Assendelft your new home, this website is here to provide you with the essential information you need.
